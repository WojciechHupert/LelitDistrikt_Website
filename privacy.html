Privacy and Cookies

Clear disclosure, minimal tracking.

This notice explains what information this site uses, which cookies are essential, and how optional analytics are controlled.

Who operates this site

The website lelitdistrikt.com promotes the Lelit Distrikt project. The site identifies Wojciech Hupert and Shape Seeds as the project publisher. For privacy questions or requests, use whupert@gmail.com.

Cookies and similar storage

This site uses a small amount of browser storage for essential functions and, only if you consent, Google Analytics for audience measurement.

Essential storage: used to remember your cookie preference so the banner does not reappear on every page load. This is necessary for the consent mechanism itself to function.

Optional analytics: Google Analytics is used to measure visits, page usage, and site performance trends. These analytics cookies are disabled unless you actively opt in.

Analytics provider

If you allow analytics, this site loads Google Analytics with measurement ID G-ZE4MF08M7X. Google Analytics may process information such as page views, approximate device and browser details, and interaction data used for aggregate reporting.

Google’s product documentation states that Analytics stores a client identifier in a first-party _ga cookie when analytics storage is enabled. Google documentation: GA4 data collection.

Legal basis

Optional analytics cookies are used on the basis of your consent. Essential storage used to remember your cookie choice is used because it is necessary for the site’s consent mechanism to work.

If you reject analytics, the site should continue to function normally without those optional cookies.

Your choices

You can accept, reject, or customize optional cookies from the banner. You can also revisit your choice later by using the persistent Cookie settings control shown on the site.

If your privacy rights request concerns personal data handled through this site, contact whupert@gmail.com.
