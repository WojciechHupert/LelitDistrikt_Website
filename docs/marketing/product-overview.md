# Product Overview (v3.3)

## What Lelit Distrikt 2 Is

Lelit Distrikt 2 is an autonomous social simulation built in Unreal Engine 5.7. It combines authored worldbuilding with local AI conversation, persistent memory, and a recursive NPC-to-NPC social engine.

The project is set inside a sealed Mediterranean district where conversation is the primary mechanic. Unlike traditional "AI chat" games, Lelit Distrikt is an ecosystem where rumors spread, secrets morph, and the district’s residents continue their social lives whether the player is there or not.

## Core Product Thesis

Most open worlds simulate scale. Lelit Distrikt 2 simulates **Autonomous Social Evolution.**

The project's differentiator is the **Closed Cognitive Loop**. Conversations become structured records, records become durable memories, and memories drive autonomous social actions. This cycle repeats recursively, allowing the district’s collective knowledge to evolve organically.

## What Makes It Distinct (v3.3)

- **Autonomous Hops**: Information propagates across the district via 10-hop social chains.
- **Local-Only Privacy**: Total local inference (Ollama/Kokoro/Whisper) with zero cloud dependency.
- **Structured Fact Extraction**: Moves beyond prose into data-driven FACT: reporting for high memory fidelity.
- **Unwavering Authenticity**: A strict technical mandate ensures NPCs remain residents, never "assistants."
- **Glass-Box Telemetry**: A professional studio dashboard for monitoring the district’s neural vitals in real-time.

## Current Product Shape

Today, the project is a technically complete autonomous social simulation.
- **Recursive social pipeline**: NPCs talk, reflect, and remember without player input.
- **Multi-layered memory**: Durable world-facts, personal identities, and relationship tensors.
- **High-fidelity UI**: A professional Next.js 14 dashboard for simulation control.
- **Unified Time Standard**: System-wide synchronization on UTC for flawless event continuity.

## What It Is Not Yet

It is not a traditional RPG with scripted quest logs, nor is it a mass-market consumer product. It is a high-end experimental simulation platform for exploring the future of autonomous narrative and digital ethics.
