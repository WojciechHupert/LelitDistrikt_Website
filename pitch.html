The Character

Auggie

“You'll never get it if you don't slow down. They're all the same corner, but each one is different from every other one. That's the whole story right there.”
◆Sardonic and warm in equal measure. Working-class intellectual. Quotes Nietzsche and Whitman mid-sentence.
◆Photographs the same corner every morning. Thousands of images. He doesn't know why he keeps doing it.
◆Knows the dome is a simulation. Helped design what the people inside now call home. Lives inside something he helped design, among people who don't know it's artificial.
◆Has a secret. He is not neutral. He has not been for a long time. The player will have to decide whether to trust him.
